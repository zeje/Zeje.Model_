打包步骤
一、给所有的csproj文件添加如下语句
  <Import Project="$(MSBuildBinPath)\Microsoft.Data.Entity.targets" Condition="Exists('$(MSBuildBinPath)\Microsoft.Data.Entity.targets')"/>
二、打开目录Zeje.Model_\.nuget，将NuGet.xml文件放到此目录中
三、打开目录Zeje.Model_\.nuget，从目录地址栏中输入cmd
四、打开命令行后，拷贝如下命令执行

nuget pack ..\Zeje.Model_.EF_.Hr_\Zeje.Model_.EF_.Hr_.csproj -Build -Properties Configuration=Release
nuget pack ..\Zeje.Model_.EF_.QY_\Zeje.Model_.EF_.QY_.csproj -Build -Properties Configuration=Release
nuget pack ..\Zeje.Model_.EF_.Sys_\Zeje.Model_.EF_.Sys_.csproj -Build -Properties Configuration=Release

nuget pack ..\Zeje.Model_.Dapper_.Hr_\Zeje.Model_.Dapper_.Hr_.csproj -Build -Properties Configuration=Release
nuget pack ..\Zeje.Model_.Dapper_.QY_\Zeje.Model_.Dapper_.QY_.csproj -Build -Properties Configuration=Release
nuget pack ..\Zeje.Model_.Dapper_.Sys_\Zeje.Model_.Dapper_.Sys_.csproj -Build -Properties Configuration=Release

nuget push *.nupkg -s http://www.zeje.cn:8080 zeje123!@#
